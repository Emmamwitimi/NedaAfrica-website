OUR PARTNERS

Kianda School

Kianda is a school in Kenya that embraces the main fundamental aspects of a person’s development: Intellectual, Spiritual, and Human. Kianda assists with books that we use to distribute to schools in marginalized areas through the project E-MAKTABA.

More Information

Strathmore School

Strathmore School is a private, independent, boys’ day school located in Nairobi, Kenya. The School’s chief purpose is to develop the minds and consciences of its students so that they can become self-disciplined and resourceful, and therefore better able to serve society in a useful way.

More Information

Grace Children’s Home Mwatate

Grace Mwatate Children’s Home and Rescue Centre is a voluntary charitable institution, providing a home for more than forty vulnerable and orphaned children aged 3-18. Currently, we do mentorship and Saturday programs in this institution assisted by local university students.

Katerina Agencies

Katerina Agencies is a clothing and design company that supplies uniforms and designer clothes to individuals and organizations. Most of the overhead costs of running NEDA activities are catered by Katerina Agencies.

More Information

Yasinga Sky Lounge

Yasinga Skye Lounge is a restaurant that offers a unique dining experience at Tsavo Apartments. Yasinga Skye Lounge through its charity Sunday raises money that sponsors school fees for selected needy students under project E-Maktaba.
