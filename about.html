About Us

Supporting NEDA

NEDA advocates a holistic approach to personal growth, emphasizing the balance between nature and nurture, education and enlightenment, dream and action, and ability and attitude. It encourages individuals to embrace their authentic selves while blending academic knowledge with real-world experiences to cultivate wisdom. NEDA stresses the importance of setting clear goals and taking strategic actions to achieve dreams, recognizing that both ability and attitude are essential for success. By integrating these principles, NEDA provides a framework for individuals to realize their full potential and pursue fulfillment in all aspects of life.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To facilitate a platform that engages individuals from different backgrounds efficiently and effectively so as to always ensure a positive effect not only to individuals but the society as a whole and hence create a positive ripple.

Vision

To ensure an individual’s internal growth and self-awareness.

Our History

NEDA in Taita (a Kenyan dialect) means to blossom, but a bit further in another region of Taita, Kasighau, the same word means absent-mindedness or getting lost in daydreams, which are in a way two opposites of the same word. One cannot blossom if they spend time daydreaming. However, we as NEDA Africa use this as a way of meshing two opposite inner personal traits to bring out a holistic experience in a human being. How can one use their dreams to blossom? NEDA seeks to be that bridge. Looking at other international dialects, NEDA is seen to literally mean ‘Inner Voice’, or ‘Voice for God’ (ARABIC). In NEDA, we create a platform where one aims to find their intrinsic purpose by following that inner voice that unites all humanity. Following the meshing of two opposite traits.
