Our Programs

E-MAKTABA

E-MAKTABA provides mentorship and safe spaces for nurturing talents, especially in rural, slum, and marginalized areas. We set up container libraries to achieve this. Our approach involves giving individuals ample time for thinking, reflection, and collaboration. We encourage unplanned physical and intellectual activities, along with mentorship to help individuals explore their interests and capabilities effectively.

Future Projects

UFANISI

Ufanisi is a project that is aimed to be founded on the principle of providing an empowerment platform to women aged 12-40 years in marginalized areas. Women residing in marginalized areas of Africa bear the greatest brunt of social exclusion. Ufanisi seeks to offer technical courses that would ensure ladies can economically sustain themselves while also ensuring holistic development by nurturing core values.

Learn More

SHUPAVU

Shupavu is a project that focuses on vocational and behavioral training among men led by a team of dedicated mentors who strive to bring the best out of boys and male adults. The project seeks to bridge the gap left by the loss of identity in men in the current society by encouraging them to shoulder responsibilities while upholding key virtues such as integrity, honesty, kindness, and hard work.

ANTHERA

Anthera is a project that focuses on coming up with sustainable environmental and water practices while still ensuring a win-win situation is achieved with all the stakeholders. Access to clean water is a basic human right, but unfortunately, it is not always available to everyone especially those in marginalized areas. In many parts of the world, people struggle to access clean water for their daily needs, and this issue is only expected to worsen due to climate change and increasing population. Anthera therefore is a project that aims to address this problem among others by implementing sustainable water solutions that can provide clean water to those in dire need of it.
